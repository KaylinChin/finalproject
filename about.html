About The Site!

On this site, you can now create and keep your vinyl record collection online!

Why is this useful?

As you continue to collect and add to your collection, it may become to large to manage on paper! So this site gives you the oppertunity to manage your collection digitally, and in a different format than you may see in different places!

Features

Add a new record to your collection through the "Add New" in the navigation area. There you will find different places to input information about your record! The only information that is required is album title and the artist's name! Then there is a place to upload photos of the gatefold!
